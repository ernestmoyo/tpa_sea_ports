## PART I: APPLICATION AND INTERPRETATIONS

### Scope
Applies to all sea ports in Part I of the Second Schedule to the Ports Act Cap. 166.

### Key Definitions

- **Change of Status**: Amendment of original container/cargo status declared in manifest to a new status.
- **Coastwise Cargo**: Cargo carried between ports in the Second Schedule along Tanzania's coastal waters.
- **Coasters (Ships Engaged in Coastal Trade)**: Ships registered in Tanzania trading between Mogadishu (North), Maputo (South), and Port Victoria (East).
- **Dangerous Cargo**: Substances listed in the IMDG Code (IMO). Must be declared by class on all clearance documents. Trade names not acceptable.
- **Deadweight Tonne (DWT)**: 1,000 kilograms.
- **Destuffing/Stripping**: Unloading cargo from a container.
- **Difficult Cargo**: Special cargo requiring extra/specialized handling (charcoal, sulphur, wet hides, pig iron, etc.).
- **Direct Delivery**: Cargo discharged from ship directly onto consignee's truck/wagon without port storage.
- **Direct Loading**: Cargo loaded onto ship from consignee's truck/wagon without port storage.
- **Domestic**: Goods produced, permanently entered, or manufactured within Tanzania.
- **Dry Port/ICD**: Inland intermodal terminal connected by road/rail to a seaport for transshipment of sea cargo.
- **Harbour Tonne (HTN)**: 1,000 kg or 1 cubic metre, whichever yields the higher charge.
- **Shorehandling**: Handling, transfer, or removal of cargo between quay/jetty and transit sheds/warehouses/stacking yards.
- **Stevedoring**: Transfer or movement of cargo within the vessel and/or between vessel and quay or next transport mode.
- **Stuffing**: Loading and arranging cargo in a container.
- **Traditional Vessel**: Under 200 GRT, locally built/rigged or majority Tanzanian crew.
- **Valuable Cargo**: Bullion, curios, ivory, essential oils, goldware, hippo teeth, platinum, precious stones, pyrethrum, rhino horns, silks, silverware, specie, tortoise shells, ad valorem B/L cargo.
- **Wharfage**: Charge on all cargo (incl. empty containers) passing over TPA quays, wharves, jetties, and buoys.

### Ports Covered
Bagamoyo, Dar es Salaam, Kilwa Kivinje, Kwale, Lindi, Mtwara & Mikindani, Pangani, Tanga.

## PART II: APPLICABLE PRINCIPLES

### 1. Tonnage Scale
- All imports/exports: assessed on tonne (1,000 kg) or 1 cubic metre, whichever yields higher charge.
- Containerised cargo: charged per TEU.
- Valuable items (gold, platinum, precious stones, etc.): per USD 200 CIF value.
- Specific heavy materials (cement, steel, fertiliser, etc.): per tonne of 1,000 kg.
- Rounding: amounts of 1-99 cents rounded to USD 1.00.
- Undercharges/overcharges not exceeding USD 0.05 shall not be raised.

### 2. Working Hours

| Service | Regular Hours (Mon-Fri) | Overtime |
|---------|------------------------|----------|
| Receipt of Import/Export Documents | 0700-2300 | Sat/Sun/Public Holidays |
| Receipt and Delivery of Cargo | 0700-0700 (24hr) | — |
| Repairing broken packages | 0700-1800 | — |
| Shorehandling/Stevedoring 1st Shift | 0700-1500 | — |
| Shorehandling/Stevedoring 2nd Shift | 1500-2300 | — |
| Shorehandling/Stevedoring 3rd Shift | 2300-0700 | Always overtime |
| Conservancy & Water Supply | 0700-1200 and 1300-1600 | — |
| Others | 0700-1500 | — |
| Weekends & Public Holidays | All overtime | All overtime |

### Invoice Cut-off
Customers can pay and clear cargo on same working day as invoice printed, or within 24 hours, without additional storage charges.

### 3. Dangerous Cargo Surcharges (Global Rules)

| Surcharge Type | Rate | Applies To |
|----------------|------|------------|
| DG — Stevedoring | +10% | Clauses 14, 36, 37, 38 (loose or containerised) |
| DG — Shorehandling | +10% | Clause 29 (loose or containerised) |
| DG — Storage | +20% | Clause 32 (after 24hr free period) |
| Over-dimension containers | +30% | Clauses 29, 36, 37, 38 |
| Cold storage cargo handling | +30% | Clause 29 (loose, stripping, or stuffing) |

### 4. Container Rules
- **FCL Ship**: Determined by Port Director before vessel starts working.
- **Through B/L Container**: House-to-house; handling + wharfage paid by ship's agents.
- **Reefer Containers**: Plugged to discharge points; charged per Clause 39.
- **Container Status**: Must be declared in manifest. Undeclared = storage charges from landing.
- **VGM (SOLAS)**: Shipper must provide verified gross mass before vessel loading.
- **Heavy lift charges**: Do not apply to containers.
- **Stuffing deadline**: Export cargo received 4 working days before vessel works. Empty containers 5 days before.
